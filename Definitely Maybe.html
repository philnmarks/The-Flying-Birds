Release Notes

Definitely Maybe, released in 1994, is the groundbreaking debut album from Oasis that set the stage for the Britpop era. At a time when grunge dominated the music scene, Oasis offered a fresh and unapologetic sound that was filled with confidence and ambition.The album includes iconic tracks like "Rock 'n' Roll Star," "Live Forever," and "Cigarettes & Alcohol," which resonated deeply with the working-class youth of the UK. With Noel Gallagher's sharp songwriting and Liam Gallagher's distinctive, raw vocals, Definitely Maybe became an instant classic, embodying the defiance and hope of a generation.The success of the album helped to redefine British rock, inspiring a resurgence that hadn't been seen since the days of The Beatles and The Rolling Stones. Its influence extended beyond music, positioning Oasis as the leaders of the Britpop movement, alongside bands like Blur and Pulp.Nearly three decades later, Definitely Maybe continues to be hailed as one of the greatest debut albums in music history, maintaining its place as a timeless masterpiece that shaped British music and inspired countless artists to follow.

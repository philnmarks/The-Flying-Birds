Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds

Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds is the solo project of Noel Gallagher, former lead guitarist and songwriter of the iconic British rock band Oasis. Formed in 2010, the band blends rock, pop, and electronic influences with Gallagher's signature songwriting style. Known for hit songs like "AKA... What a Life!" and "In the Heat of the Moment," the band has released multiple albums that have topped UK charts, showcasing Gallagher's continued influence in modern rock music.
